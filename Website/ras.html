Ras Malai

Ingredients:

  • we need 12 to 15 rasgulla
  • 1 liter whole milk
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1 cup sugar
  • peel the almonds and slice
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 2 tablespoons chopped pistachios
  • 1 litre of full fat whole milk in a thick bottomed pan or kadai.
  • 2 tablespoons chopped almonds
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup condensed milk

Instructions: Boil the milk in a pan. Once it boils, add lemon juice to curdle the milk. Strain the curdled milk using a muslin clothRasmalai is made by soaking or cooking rasgulla in thickened milk for a few minutes. The rasgulla balls absorb the flavor of the sweetened flavored milk and becomes soft. These soft balls are served together with the sweetened thickened milk.
