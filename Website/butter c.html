Butter Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 500g Raw Chicken cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 tsp Red Chilli Powder
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon red chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon turmeric powder
  • 1 tablespoon garam masala
  • 1 tablespoon cumin powder
  • 1 tablespoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 cup Curd
  • 2 tsp Kasuri Methi
  • 2 tsp Mustard Oil

Instructions:In a mixing bowl, put raw chicken pieces and add salt, red chilli powder, ginger garlic paste and lemon juice. Mix well.2.Refrigerate for about 15-20 minutes3.Now add curd to the refrigerated mix. Followed by salt, ginger garlic paste, red chilli powder, garam masala, kasuri methi and mustard oil. Mix well and refrigerate again for an hour.4.Roast the marinated chicken in an oven for about 30 minutes until it is three-fourth done.. ...
