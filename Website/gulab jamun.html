Gulab Jamun

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup ghee (clarified butter)
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • For Sugar Syrup:
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • Rose water (optional)
  • Saffron strands (optional)
  • Oil or ghee for frying

Instructions: In a mixing bowl, combine milk powder, all-purpose flour, ghee, milk, and baking soda. Mix well to form a smooth, soft dough. Divide the dough into small portions and shape them into round balls...
