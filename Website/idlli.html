Idli

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups idli rice
  • 1 cup urad dal (black gram)
  • 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste

Instructions: Rinse idli rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds separately. Soak them in water for 4-6 hours. Grind the urad dal and fenugreek seeds into a smooth batter. Grind the idli rice into a slightly coarse batter...
