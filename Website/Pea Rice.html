Pea Rice

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 cup peas
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
