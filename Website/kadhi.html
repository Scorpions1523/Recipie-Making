Kadhi

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1/4 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tablespoon ghee
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon asafoetida (hing)
  • Curry leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions: Whisk together yogurt, gram flour, and water to make a smooth mixture. In a pan, heat ghee, add cumin seeds, mustard seeds, fenugreek seeds, and let them splutter...
